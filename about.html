We are Yomi Creatives

A proudly Eastern Cape design studio and software engineering start-up building the digital future of South African healthcare.

Yomi Creatives was born in the Eastern Cape with a single mission: to give every South African medical practice the same world-class digital presence that large private hospitals enjoy — at a price every practice can afford. Our R1000 Complete Package includes a full professional website plus every core software module — booking, blog, committee pages, and patient portal — for a single once-off investment.
